Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all personal data by following these steps within the app:

  1. Open the Tilswall app
  2. Go to Me in the bottom navigation bar
  3. Tap the Settings icon in the top right corner
  4. Select Delete Account
  5. Enter your password and follow the on-screen instructions to complete the process

Once deleted, all your data will be permanently removed from our servers.
